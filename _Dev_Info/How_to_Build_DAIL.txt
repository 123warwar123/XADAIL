	[DAIL] Build instructions.

****************************************************************
****************************************************************


1. Decompress the contents of VanillaBak\VanillaBak.7z  into the same folder where you see it or the NSIS files won't compile.

2. Clean out those pesky resources folders with "KLEANJUNK.bat"

3. Propogate any files that i forgot to with  "SKILLMERGER.bat" from the root of \mods\

4. Load the asset manager

5. Go through every mod that has "DAIL" at the start and hit F7 on each

6. After each mod is compiled, choose archive -> compact

7. Once this is done run "GQMERGE.bat" from the root of \mods\

8. Load NSIS

9. Load DAIL.nsi and hit CTRL+F9

10. Repeat for DAIL-LITE.nsi

11. Thats it you have built DAIL.  Upload it to nexus and tell the world of its arrival.
